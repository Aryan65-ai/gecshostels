About Our Hostel

Welcome to the Government Engineering College Samastipur Boys Hostel - a home away from home for our students. We are committed to providing a safe, comfortable, and conducive environment for academic excellence and personal growth.

Our Mission

To provide quality accommodation and support services that enhance the educational experience of our students while fostering a sense of community, discipline, and academic excellence.

Our Vision

To be a leading hostel facility that creates an environment where students can thrive academically, develop life skills, and build lasting friendships while maintaining the highest standards of safety and comfort.
