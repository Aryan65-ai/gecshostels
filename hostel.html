Hostel Overview

Our hostel provides safe, secure and affordable accommodation for students of Government Engineering College. Room availability can be viewed on the homepage.

Facilities

  • 24x7 Security and CCTV surveillance
  • Wi‑Fi enabled study rooms
  • RO drinking water and hot water access
  • Shared laundry area
  • Mess with vegetarian meals (see Fees for charges)

Floors & Room Types

Floors

  • Floor 4: Single rooms
  • Floor 3: Single rooms
  • Floor 2: Single & Triple rooms
  • Floor 1: Triple rooms

Rules

  • Maintain discipline and cleanliness in rooms and common areas
  • Quiet hours after 10:00 PM
  • No cooking electrical appliances inside rooms
  • Visitors are allowed only in designated hours
  • Any issues should be reported via i‑Complain on the homepage

Mess

Nutritious vegetarian meals served three times a day. Mess committee includes student representatives.
